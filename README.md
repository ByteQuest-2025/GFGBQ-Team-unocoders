### Overview
EarlySignal is a full-stack healthcare risk assessment system designed to detect silent or early-stage diseases such as diabetes and heart disease using machine learning.  
The project focuses on predictive prevention by generating risk probability scores instead of binary outcomes, helping identify health risks before clinical diagnosis.

### Problem Statement
A large percentage of life-threatening diseases remain undiagnosed for years because:
* Symptoms are mild or ignored  
* Health data is fragmented  
* Healthcare systems rely on reactive diagnosis  

EarlySignal addresses this gap by enabling early risk detection, explainable insights, and preventive awareness.
